What is
Psychotherapy?

During psychotherapy, you learn about your condition and your moods, feelings, thoughts and behaviors. Psychotherapy helps you learn how to take control of your life and respond to challenging situations with healthy coping skills.


In some cases, psychotherapy can be as effective as medications, such as antidepressants.

Benefits

Psychotherapy can be helpful in treating most mental health problems, including:

  • Anxiety disorders
  • Mood disorders
  • Addictions
  • Eating disorders
  • Personality disorders
  • Schizophrenia
  • And improve your life in general

Who am I?

Dr Olivia June is a specialist adult psychiatrist and is active in her private practice in Paris since 2014. She works predominantly with the international community of expats, students and diplomats. She has particular expertise in the treatment of
- Anxiety disorders and OCD
- Mood disorders, depression and bipolar disorder
- Burnout and other stress related disorders

Qualifications:

  • Psychologist: Licensed in California
  • Marriage and Family Therapist: Licensed in California
  • Psychologist: Licensed in Oregon
  • Clinical Experience: Over eight years
  • Adjunct Faculty: Alliant University—San Diego
  • Training Workshops: Conducted for Psychotherapists in Individual and Couples Therapy in: Brazil, Canada, England, Mexico, Norway, Scotland, United States, Wales
